Description

This chicken sandwich is the perfect blend of juicy, crispy chicken layered on a toasted bun with fresh toppings and a creamy, tangy sauce. A golden-brown, seasoned chicken breast is fried or air-fried to perfection, then stacked with crisp lettuce, ripe tomato slices, pickles, and a generous spread of garlic mayo or spicy aioli. Whether you serve it on a brioche bun or toasted sourdough, this sandwich delivers bold flavor and satisfying crunch in every bite.
